About Revolution

WE CREATE
MEMORIES
THAT LAST

Revolution Events Ltd is a passionate, experienced, and perfection-driven organization headquartered in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. With over two decades of industry leadership, we have redefined what's possible in live event production across East Africa.

From corporate soirées and international celebrity concerts to product launches and grand stadium festivals — our pioneering innovative spirit sets us apart and keeps us at the top of our industry.

Our commitment to on-time, on-budget delivery paired with top-notch visual experiences is what turns every event into a landmark occasion.
